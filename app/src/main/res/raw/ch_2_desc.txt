After the death of Sheikh Jassim Bin Mohammed Bin Thani, his son Sheikh Abdullah Bin Jassim Al Thani became the ruler.
After three years of his rule, he signed a treaty with Britain, in order to bring Qatar under its Trucial System of Administration.
On 30 June 1948, Sheikh Abdullah appointed his son Sheikh Ali Bin Abdullah as the Deputy Ruler following the 
death of his brother the heir apparent Sheikh Hamad bin Abdullah on 27 May 1948.
